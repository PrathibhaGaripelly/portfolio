About

I am a theoretical physicist widely regarded as one of history's greatest and most influential physicists. I not only developed the theory of relativity, but I also made significant contributions to the theory of quantum mechanics.

Experience

  • Got job as technical assistant at Swiss Patent Office in 1902
  • In 1908,I was appointed as a lecturer at the University of Bern.
  • In 1914, I was appointed to the Prussian Academy of Sciences.
  • In 1917, I began advocating for peace and social justice.
